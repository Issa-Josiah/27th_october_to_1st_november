Tigers are the largest cats, known for their powerful build and unique striped coats, and are found only in Asia. These solitary, carnivorous predators are also strong swimmers, with unique stripe patterns that provide camouflage. They are apex predators that hunt prey like deer, wild pigs, and buffalo, sometimes consuming up to \(40\) kilograms of meat in a single meal.
